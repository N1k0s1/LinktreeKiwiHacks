## How to Edit

Each link follows this format:
```
- [Link Title](https://url.com) | image-url | featured
```

- **Link Title**: Text shown on the button
- **URL**: Where the link goes
- **Image URL**: URL to an icon/image (use a small square image)
- **featured**: Add this word to make the button tan/highlighted (optional)

### Examples

```markdown
- [My Website](https://example.com) | https://example.com/icon.png | featured
- [YouTube](https://youtube.com/@channel) | https://youtube.com/favicon.ico
```
